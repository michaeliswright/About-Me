About myself

I was born in the srping of 1999, March 28th, In Long Beach California. My Father, a natural born citizen of the States, and my mother, born in Cambodia.

My fathers profession was a teacher, taking up in bilingual education. He took his studies from California to Arizona, and after earning his PHD, moved to San Antonio Texas.

Texas was the state I concider myself grown up in, It is where I spend ten years of my life, started my education, and made plenty of friends along the way.

Time had pasted, and one could see themselved remaining in Texas, but my father wished to push his carrear, and got a job as a Professor at Purdue University.

Depite protest from most of the family, we moved to Indiana, and have remained here for quiet the time.
